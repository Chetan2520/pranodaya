At Pranodaya, we tackle the silent threat of dust pollution through innovative recycling technologies. By capturing industrial and urban dust and converting it into safe, usable construction materials, we turn an environmental hazard into a resource. Our process reduces airborne particulate matter, directly improving public health and ecosystem stability.

Activities & Features

One of our key challenges was developing a filtration system efficient enough for high-density industrial zones yet cost-effective for community deployment. Through rigorous R&D, we achieved a balance that maximizes impact.

  • Advanced filtration for high-pollution zones.

  • Community-led air quality monitoring.

  • Repurposing dust into eco-bricks.

  • Planting green buffers to trap dust naturally.

  • Advocating for stricter emission norms.

  • Health camps for affected communities.
