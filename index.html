Pranodaya Foundation is an initiative to provide employment and food to the helpless people. We aim to do something for those sections of society who have no support, no roof over their heads, and no arrangement for two meals a day. Our institution is working for the economic and social improvement of such people.

Established on January 05, 2026, the foundation is a Section 8 Non-Profit Company (The Companies Act 2013).
