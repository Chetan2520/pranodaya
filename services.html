Joining us is simple! You can register as a volunteer on our website, attend an orientation session, and start contributing to our projects immediately. We strive to make onboarding as smooth as possible.

Pranodaya is a non-profit organization. We do not charge fees for volunteering. However, specific specialized training programs may have a nominal cost to cover materials and certification.

Transparency is our core value. 100% of public donations go directly towards our projects. Administrative costs are covered by specific grants and corporate sponsorships. We publish annual audited reports on our website.

We measure our impact through rigorous monitoring and evaluation, tracking metrics like the number of beneficiaries, environmental quality improvements, and direct feedback from the communities we serve.
